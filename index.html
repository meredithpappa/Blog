About Me

I've been a pianist my entire life. I originally taught at University of the Incarnate Word in San Antonio, Tx and maintained a studio of pre-collegiate students for many years. When I moved to Greece, I started off as an English teacher, but eventually grew interested in programming when my husband started a boot camp. I decided to give FreeCodeCamp a try and discovered I liked building different projects and that's how I ended up in InTechGration's bootcamp.
